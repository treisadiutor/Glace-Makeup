Our Story

The journey of GLACÉ

Company History

GLACÉ was founded in 2025 by Jacelyn Esperas, a visionary makeup artist passionate about creating luxurious and sustainable beauty. With a strong desire to redefine industry standards, we have grown from a small business to a thriving business dedicated to the beauty industry and the health of our planet. We believe that true beauty makes people and the world better.

Our Mission & Vision

We aim to embrace beauty through the transformative power of makeup, honor each individuals unique sensibility, and help them express their true selves. We believe beauty products should be high quality, ethical, and sustainable — and consistent with this belief, we want to inspire a community of confident, empowered individuals who share our passion for beauty. Beauty for us is an experience and we try to convey that experience in every product we produce.

Our Process

Each of our creation is the result of a deep understanding of what our clients' needs. From concept to the final product, we combine centuries-old technology with innovative approaches. Every ingredient is uniquely curated to not just achieve the best-quality results, but to create a pleasurable process that feels just as good as it looks. We are committed to creating not just makeup, but splendid moments that is both empowering and delighting.

Our Brand Commitment

Our commitment to sustainability is invaluable, from the ingredients that are responsibly sourced to our recyclable packaging. We are vehemently anti-animal testing, and we ensure complete transparency so you will know exactly the content in every single product on your vanity. We celebrate diversity and strive to create a brand where everyone feels included as part of our conscious community committed to envisioning the world as a more beautiful place.
